A good drywall repair is built in stages. The patch needs solid backing, the joints need the right reinforcement, and each coat has to be shaped with the final wall or ceiling in mind. I repair everything from doorknob holes and anchor damage to larger access cuts and ceiling patches, then blend the texture and finish so the repair stops drawing attention.

Why Homeowners Choose Me

Solid patching methods selected for the size and location of the damage
Careful feathering and sanding to avoid a visible mound around the repair
Texture blending for common San Diego wall and ceiling finishes
Floors, furniture, and nearby surfaces protected from dust and compound
Paint-ready finishing with touch-up painting available when practical
